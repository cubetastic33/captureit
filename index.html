Capture it

Welcome to Capture it! The objective of this game is to use your tiles to surround all enemy tiles (in red) in right, left, top, and bottom sides with your ally tiles (in blue). Simple, right? All tiles can move any number of tiles in right, left, top, or bottom directions. You are allowed to play with a friend, with whom you can communicate using chat.
